Common pool resource game

Rules

You and other members are managing a shared resource. At the beginning, there are a number of units of resource in the pool.

In each round, each of the member in the group will decide how much to take from pool. Decisions will be made independent and unbeknownst to each other. This means each member knows only his/her own decision and the collective outcome of the whole group.

After each round, the remaining resources in the pool will be calculated. If at least 50% of the units remains in the pool, the remaining resources will be multiplied by 1.5 and the game continues to the next round. However, if the resources fall below 50%, the game ends

You will be rewarded proportionate to the units your earned from the game, so please keep track of your paid-off
