Common pool resources game

You and other members are managing a shared resource. There are a number of units of resource endowed in the pool at the beginning.

In each round, each member in the group (including you) will decide how much to take from pool. Decisions will be made independent and unbeknownst to each other. This means each member knows only his/her own decision and the collective outcome of the whole group.

After each round, the remaining resources in the pool will be calculated. If at least 50% of the units compared to the original endownment remains in the pool, the remaining resources will be multiplied by 1.5 and the game continues to the next round.

However, if the resources fall below 50%, the game ends

You will be rewarded proportionate to the units your earned from the game, so please keep track of your paid-off
