What is it?

The digit spin test was designed to assess your working memory. A testperson is visually or auditorily exposed to a sequence of digits one after the other. Right afterwards, the test subject has to recall the correct digits in the same order. Most people manage to recap around seven digits.


  1. Forward digit span test

    2. In this game, the players will recall the digits in the same order they were presented

  2. Backward digit span test

    3. In this game, the players will recall the digits in the reversed order they were presented
